The route, step by step

  1. St. Peter's Basilica — the start; the Vatican's great dome
  2. Castel Sant'Angelo — Hadrian's riverside fortress
  3. Ponte Sant'Angelo — the angel-lined bridge over the Tiber
  4. Piazza Navona — Bernini's Fountain of the Four Rivers
  5. Campo de' Fiori — the lively market square
  6. Theatre of Marcellus — the ancient "mini-Colosseum"
  7. Tiber Island — the little island midstream
  8. Mouth of Truth — Bocca della Verità at Santa Maria in Cosmedin
  9. Circus Maximus — the finish; Rome's ancient chariot track

The route, step by step

  1. Aventine Keyhole — the start; the Knights of Malta's secret view of St. Peter's dome
  2. Giardino degli Aranci — the Orange Garden terrace over Rome
  3. Bocca della Verità — the Mouth of Truth at Santa Maria in Cosmedin
  4. Circus Maximus — the ancient chariot track
  5. Colosseum — the Flavian Amphitheatre
  6. Arch of Constantine — the great triumphal arch
  7. Roman Forum — the heart of ancient Rome, along Via dei Fori Imperiali
  8. Piazza del Campidoglio — the finish; Michelangelo's square atop the Capitoline

The route, step by step

  1. Piazza Venezia — the start; the great white Vittoriano monument
  2. Pantheon — the best-preserved temple of ancient Rome
  3. Piazza Colonna — the Column of Marcus Aurelius
  4. Trevi Fountain — toss a coin over your shoulder
  5. Piazza di Spagna — the Spanish Steps & Barcaccia fountain
  6. Pincio Terrace — Piazzale Napoleone I — the panoramic terrace above the square
  7. Piazza del Popolo — the finish; the grand oval with its obelisk
